Fun Elephant Facts

  • Elephants can live for up to 70 years
  • The African elephant is the largest of all land mammals
  • Elephants are able to swim for long distances and walk about 4 mph
  • Elephants consume 300-500 pounds of food per day
  • They live in tight social units that are led by an older matriarch
  • They have the largest brains in the the animal kingdom
